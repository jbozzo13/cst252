My Pop Up

Welcome to My Pop Up!



My Pop Up is a pop up that reminds you to get off your computer!
We want to limit the amount of time we spend on the computer, so we set a timer that creates a pop up to remind us to get off for 30 minutes. There is a "I'm in the middle of a project button" that allows you to continue going if needed, but otherwise the popup won't go away unless you take a 30 minute break.
